Requiem

hi all I bring a game made by me, called Requiem , is the story of a sorcerer that his sister was kidnapped and also has been taken away his powers, along history, Dante the main character begins to regain his powers if Dante does not destroy Xultur on time his sister will be finalized at the hands of Xultur , since the soul of Artemis is not an ordinary soul , since she is actually a goddess who was expelled for a crime she committed and forced to relive in a human as punishment and not knowing that she was a goddess that is without memory, Xultur to complete his staff with the 5 talismans and soul of Artemis , may get the powers of the gods and create a world of their resemblance , Dante will do their best to save his sister and prevent the world from being destroyed .
